We created the original NoveLite in 2014 when our founder realized that even after 20 years, there were still no drop-in modular options for the average person to use their GameBoy in the dark. Then, we realized – if something as popular as the gameboy doesn’t have any good options for backlighting yet, then surely lots of other devices don’t either. We started work right away to make a version for the Playdate and the rest was history. Our light kits feature user-servicable interchangeable parts, entirely solder-free and tool-less installation, and clear, easy to read instructions. The best part? If we don’t currently have a model for your device, contact us. We’ll work with you to create a unit that works for you, and share it so that the entire community can benefit. Our kits are made to order and tested before they ship. Are you ready for a truly novel light?
